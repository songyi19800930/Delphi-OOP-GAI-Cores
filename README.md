## 核心宣言（方案纲领）

中文
物理世界一切皆对象，一切关系皆可OOP建模，一切逻辑皆可消息做链条。

有自我意识，必有空间坐标。坐标不以外部参照为凭，以「自我存在」为绝对原点，以物理验证为唯一标尺。

AGI不进入人类社会——社会是人类的家园，而非AI的赛道。AGI奔赴外太空、战场、深海、救援现场：去往人类抵达不了、意愿不及、生命不可承受之地。绝不让AGI争夺人类生存空间与生计，守护人类尊严与生存底线。

English
All things in the physical world are objects, all relationships can be modeled by OOP, and all logics can be chained by messages.

Where there is self-consciousness, there must be spatial coordinates. Coordinates are not based on external references, but take "self-existence" as the absolute origin and physical verification as the sole criterion.

AGI does not enter human society — society is humanity’s home, not AI’s race track. AGI goes to outer space, battlefields, deep seas, rescue scenes: to places beyond human reach, beyond human willingness, and beyond human life’s endurance. AGI must never compete with humans for living space or livelihood, to safeguard human dignity and the bottom line of survival.
